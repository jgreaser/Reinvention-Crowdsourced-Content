Module one is about the foundations of our laws today. In the beginning of the module it discuses the role of Ethics in our laws. This includes how do we as a society determine what is ethical vs what is lawful. Just because it would be seen as unethical does that alone make it unlawful? These concept will help the founders of our country create the Constitution and the Bill of Rights. The module will look at the creation of these two documents and the impact on our criminal justice system. Finally you will look at an ethical dilemma and evaluate the ethics of restricting rights for the common good vs individual rights.
